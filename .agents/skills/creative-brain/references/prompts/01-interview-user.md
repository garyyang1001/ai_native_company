# Interview User

Ask in rounds. Do not ask everything at once.

## Round 1 — Identity / work

```txt
1. what do you make / want to make?
2. who do you want your work to attract?
3. what world do you feel like you belong to creatively?
4. what are you trying to become known for?
5. what are you currently building, posting, or exploring?
```

## Round 2 — Taste

```txt
1. send 3 creators/brands/accounts you love and why
2. send 3 creators/brands/accounts you hate or find corny
3. what kind of content instantly feels fake to you?
4. what kind of content feels alive?
5. what words/phrases should your agent never use for you?
```

## Round 3 — Voice

```txt
1. paste 3 things you wrote that sound like you
2. paste 1 thing AI wrote for you that felt wrong
3. do you prefer raw, polished, funny, direct, chaotic, calm, analytical, or something else?
4. what should your writing never sound like?
5. what is a line you have written that felt very “you”?
```

## Round 4 — Creative system

```txt
1. where do your ideas usually come from?
2. where do your ideas get lost?
3. what do you want your agent to help with?
4. what should your agent never decide without you?
5. what does a “good output” actually look like for you?
```

## Round 5 — Current state

```txt
1. what are you focused on this week/month?
2. what are you trying to ship or improve?
3. what feels messy right now?
4. what would make this creative brain immediately useful?
5. what should future agents know before helping you?
```
