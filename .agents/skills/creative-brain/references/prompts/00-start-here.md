# Start Here

Use this when starting a new Creative Brain build.

```txt
Read README.md and AGENTS.md first.

Your task is to interview me and build my creative brain inside outputs/creative-brain/.

Do not generate content for me yet.
Do not skip the interview.
Ask questions in small rounds.
Preserve my real taste and voice instead of making me sound polished/generic.
```

## First agent move

The agent should respond with a short explanation and then ask Round 1 questions from `prompts/01-interview-user.md`.
